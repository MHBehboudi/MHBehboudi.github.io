Foundation Models for Neural Signal Decoding

End-to-end deep learning system: self-supervised pretraining on unlabeled biosignal data to learn transferable representations, then fine-tuned with Transformer encoders and Graph Neural Networks (capturing sensor topology) for downstream neural state classification. Designed to generalize across subjects, recording devices, and signal conditions.

about

Ph.D. candidate in Cognition & Neuroscience at UT Dallas with an Electrical Engineering foundation. I specialize in the full computational stack for physiological signals: universal data readers across formats (EDF, BDF, HDF5, BrainVision), automated preprocessing that handles cross-device variability, and foundation model architectures that learn transferable representations from unlabeled data for any downstream task.

I've built a novel cross-frequency connectivity algorithm (MDPC) adopted by multiple research groups, engineered automated pipelines processing 500+ GB of data across heterogeneous recording systems, and designed a clinical diagnostic system achieving 95% accuracy from single-channel recordings. My work spans lab-grade and portable devices, pediatric through adult populations, and real-world signal conditions where nothing is clean.

Selected Work

Novel Cross-Frequency Connectivity Algorithm

Developed Time-Lagged Cross-Frequency MDPC to characterize dynamic information flow across brain networks. Full tri-language pipeline (Python, MATLAB, R) with automated preprocessing, spectral decomposition, and mixed-effects statistical modeling.

AI-Driven Sleep Apnea Diagnosis

Clinical diagnostic system using complexity features (fractals, Hurst exponent, entropy measures) with mRMR feature selection and SVM/KNN from single-channel recordings. 95% accuracy. Two peer-reviewed publications.

expertise

Technical Stack

Signal Processing & Data Engineering

Multi-channel preprocessing, artifact detection (ICA, spectral, threshold), cross-device normalization, montage mapping, time-frequency decomposition (FFT, STFT, Morlet wavelets), connectivity measures (coherence, PLV, wPLI), cross-frequency coupling, graph theory network metrics, complexity & entropy features. Foundation Models & Deep Learning

Classical ML & Statistics
