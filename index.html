About Me

I am a Ph.D. researcher in Cognition and Neuroscience with a strong foundation in Electrical Engineering. My work focuses on leveraging signal processing, statistical analysis, and engineering expertise to advance digital health products through innovative research and development. I specialize in applying computational methods and data analytics to extract meaningful insights from biosignals, with the goal of improving health outcomes.

My expertise spans MATLAB, Python, and R, and I am proficient in developing, implementing, and optimizing signal processing and machine learning pipelines for large-scale neurophysiological datasets. My background includes a focus on EEG signal processing, connectivity analysis, and developing innovative tools for real-time neural data collection and analysis.
